The creation of all-encompassing artificial intelligence powered life management systems is still a specialized field in this age of rapidly advancing Natural Language Processing (NLP) advancements. Though AI and machine learning applications are widely available, there is a noticeable lack of solutions aimed at improving everyday life via intelligent automation. This essay presents Daily Diary, a cutting-edge life management tool intended to completely transform the way people manage their everyday lives. With the goal of simplifying the intricacies of contemporary life, Daily Diary includes machine learning powered smart diaries, daily routine generators, and sophisticated financial management tools including machine learning-powered cashbooks. In this mobile application uses artificial intelligence to provide users with tailored advice on how to deal with mental health issues, improve time management, and strike a healthy balance between work and life. This app offers customized answers to common problems by utilizing advanced algorithms to examine user data. This gives users the ability to make well-informed decisions and give self-care top priority. This study examines how artificial intelligence can be revolutionary in promoting initiative-taking and conscious life management techniques, emphasizing the relationship between technology and behavior. The way that this app encourages personal development and comprehensive well-being is a paradigm change in the way that technology interacts with daily living. This study highlights the potential of artificial intelligence to improve mental and personal health management through interdisciplinary investigation, providing insights into the future of AI-driven solutions for improving human experiences.

LITERATURE SURVEY

The literature survey for the research paper encompasses a variety of studies that inform the key components of the project: emotion detection, AI-driven personalized routine management, and predicting monthly living expenses using NLP.The integration of machine learning into mobile applications for life management has shown promising results, with applications providing personalized solutions for daily challenges such as time management, mental health, and budgeting through advanced algorithms​.In the realm of emotion detection, studies like "Emotion Guru: A Smart Emotion Tracking Application with AI Conversational Agent for Exploring and Preventing Depression" highlight the rising rates of anxiety and depression and suggest AI-driven solutions for emotional self-awareness and intervention. This study achieved a 98.46% accuracy in emotion detection using machine learning models​.Further research by Song et al. presented a multi-modal physiological emotion database, emphasizing the potential of combining various physiological signals for accurate emotion recognition​.

AI-driven personalized routine management is supported by studies such as Karimanzira and Rauschenbach’s work on IoT-based predictive analytics, which underscores the importance of predictive insights for routine optimization​​. Haryanto et al.'s research on IoT in aquaponics, while focused on a different application, demonstrates the efficacy of data-driven decision-making, a concept that parallels the project's goal of optimizing daily routines through AI​​. For predicting monthly living expenses, NLP techniques are explored for automating financial tracking, as manual tracking increases the risk of errors. Research by Du et al. on deep expense extraction and classification illustrates how automated systems can facilitate efficient personal expense management by using NLP to process text from bank SMS and bill images​​. Together, these studies underscore the transformative potential of AI and machine learning in enhancing life management through personalized, data-driven insights, and highlight the ongoing advancements in emotion detection, routine optimization, and financial management.

METHODOLOGY

A. Emotion Detection Using Daily Diary and Recommendations The application leverages Natural Language Processing (NLP) methods to help users manage and reduce negative emotions. The main component is a diary feature where users record their thoughts and feelings, stored securely on Firebase. An NLP model, using the Spacy library, learns and improves its understanding of emotions over time through user-generated content. Named Entity Recognition (NER) technologies in Spacy help identify words associated with emotions, enhancing the model's emotional context comprehension. The central control hub is a Flask server hosted on the Google Cloud Platform, ensuring seamless user-NLP model interactions and scalability​​.

B. AI-Driven Personalized Routine Management and Recommendations The project employs artificial intelligence to create customized daily schedules based on user responses to an informative and entertaining questionnaire. The methodology combines strategic techniques with state-of-the-art technologies, including recurrent neural networks (RNNs) and NLP techniques. Transformer-based models like T5, BERT, and GPT are used for their capacity to understand and produce human-like text, enhancing the program's linguistic awareness and ability to create personalized routines​​.

C. Predict Monthly Living Expenses Using NLP and Geminai API Key The research integrates advanced NLP techniques with automatic text extraction from bank SMS to predict monthly living expenses. The methodology involves collecting and preparing a dataset of bill images and bank SMS, using OCR technology for text extraction, and employing NLP algorithms to identify financial data. Machine learning models classify transactions and link expenses to specific locations, creating a comprehensive user-focused mobile application interface​​.

RESEARCH PROBLEM

The research problem focuses on the challenges individuals face in managing negative emotions and personal finances effectively. It aims to develop a mobile application that provides personalized emotional support and financial management tools, leveraging advanced technologies like NLP and AI to address these issues comprehensively​​.

  • The primary research problem in emotion detection is the need for accurate, real-time identification of emotional states using machine learning algorithms within mobile applications. Existing solutions often lack the precision and context-awareness required to effectively support mental health, particularly in detecting nuanced emotions like anxiety and depression from daily user interactions and social media posts .
  • The main research problem here is developing an AI-driven system that can dynamically adapt to individual user preferences and behavioral patterns to optimize daily routines. Current mobile applications for routine management often fail to provide truly personalized recommendations due to limited predictive insights and inadequate understanding of user behavior .
  • The research problem focuses on creating a reliable, automated system to predict monthly living expenses using Natural Language Processing (NLP). Many people still rely on manual methods to track their expenses, which are prone to errors and inefficiencies. There is a significant need for a solution that can accurately classify and analyze financial data extracted from texts like bank SMS and bill images to facilitate better personal financial management .

Main Objective

The main objective is to create a comprehensive mobile application that helps users manage negative emotions and personal finances. The application aims to detect emotions, provide personalized routine recommendations, and predict monthly living expenses using sophisticated NLP and AI techniques​​.

Sub Objective 1

Developing an emotion detection system using daily diaries and NLP to provide personalized recommendations for emotional well-being.


Sub Objective 2

Creating an AI-driven personalized routine management system to enhance daily task efficiency.


Sub Objective 3

Predicting monthly living expenses through NLP and text extraction from financial documents and bank SMS, ensuring data privacy and security​​.

RESEARCH GAP

The research addresses the critical need for innovative solutions in managing personal finances and enhancing emotional well-being through technology. It highlights the persistent economic crisis in Sri Lanka, which has made personal cost management increasingly challenging, emphasizing the importance of developing automated methods to reduce manual financial tracking and improve accuracy​​.

Some Key Benifits

The mobile application developed through this research offers substantial benefits by seamlessly integrating advanced technologies to address personal and societal challenges. It enhances emotional well-being by accurately detecting and managing negative emotions using NLP and machine learning, providing real-time self-awareness and personalized support. The app also optimizes daily routines through AI-driven personalized schedules, improving productivity and efficiency by adapting to individual behaviors. Furthermore, it automates financial management by predicting monthly living expenses with NLP, reducing manual tracking errors and fostering better financial decision-making. Overall, this innovative mobile app improves mental health, enhances routine management, and supports financial stability, significantly contributing to individual quality of life and societal well-being.

Mental Health Support The application can offer tailored recommendations and interventions based on detected emotional states, supporting users in maintaining mental wellness and reducing the risk of severe mental health issues.

Better Financial Management By providing accurate insights into spending patterns, the application helps users make informed financial decisions, potentially leading to better budgeting and savings.

Personalized Daily Schedules Using AI-driven insights, the application creates personalized routines that align with individual preferences and behavioral patterns. This can enhance productivity and efficiency by optimizing time management.
